This Privacy Policy describes how the application "Thing List" (hereinafter referred to as the "App"), developed by LUKONICHEV DMITRII (hereinafter referred to as the "Developer"), processes user information.

1. Collection and Use of Information

The App does not collect or transfer your personal data to third parties. All lists, item names, and quantities you create are stored exclusively locally on your device. The Developer does not have access to this data.

2. Advertising

To maintain the free version, the App may use an advertising SDK (Petal Ads). This SDK may collect non-personal technical information about your device (such as model, operating system) and behavioral data to display relevant advertisements. This data is processed in accordance with Huawei's privacy policy.
For more details: https://consumer.huawei.com/en/privacy/privacy-policy/

By using the App, you consent to the advertising SDK processing your data in accordance with this Policy.

3. User Rights

You may delete the App at any time using the standard method of your operating system. Upon deletion, all lists, items, and settings you have created in the App will be deleted. Data that may have been transmitted to the advertising SDK (Petal Ads) is processed in accordance with Huawei's privacy policy and cannot be deleted by uninstalling the App.

You may also clear the App's data at any time through your device settings, or withdraw your consent to data processing by preventing the App from accessing your device's advertising identifier.

4. Policy Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. We recommend that you periodically review this page.
